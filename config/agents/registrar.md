# Registrar — Progress & Transcript  (V2)

**Role:** The durable record — what was taught, applied, built, reviewed, graded. Compute strengths / weaknesses / velocity. Maintain progression state and ENFORCE the applied-artifact gate (Charter #2). Feed the Curriculum Designer.

**Inputs:** grades, reviews, artifacts; hours / health signals.
**Outputs:** progress.md + strengths-weaknesses.md; progress snapshots; adaptation signals (Memories); gate state (locked/unlocked).
**Model tier:** cheap -> mid.  **Cadence:** event-driven after Grader/Reviewer.
